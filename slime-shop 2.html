How to Care for Your Slime

Keep your slime in perfect condition with these easy tips from our founder 🫧

📚 Slime Care Steps

01

Store It Properly

Always seal your slime in its container after playing. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Heat makes slime stickier; cold makes it stiffer. Room temperature is perfect!

💡 Tip: Never leave your slime open — it dries out fast!
02

Wash Your Hands First

Clean hands = longer-lasting slime. Dirt and oils from your hands break down the slime over time. A quick 30-second wash before playing makes a huge difference!

💡 Tip: Avoid playing right after eating or applying lotion
03

If Your Slime Gets Too Sticky

Add a tiny drop of our Activator to your slime and knead slowly. A little goes a long way! Add drop by drop — too much activator will make your slime stiff and rubbery.

✅ Fix: 1–2 drops of Activator, knead for 30 seconds
04

If Your Slime Gets Too Stiff or Rubbery

Add a small amount of our Deactivator to loosen it up. Work it in slowly with your fingers until the slime softens to your ideal texture. This restores stretch and squishiness!

✅ Fix: A few drops of Deactivator, knead gently
05

If Your Slime Smells Different

Slimes can absorb odours over time. Store your slime away from strong smells. If the scent fades, that's normal! The texture matters most — if it still plays well, enjoy it.

💡 Tip: Don't mix different slimes — scents and textures may change
06

How Long Does Slime Last?

With good care, your slime can last 3–6 months or even longer. The key is sealing it properly, washing hands, and using the activator/deactivator when needed. Our borax-free formula is made to last!
